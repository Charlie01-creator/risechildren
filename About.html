About Us

Empowering children in Africa, one life at a time.

Our Mission

At RISE, we believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive. Our mission is to provide essential support and resources to underprivileged children in Africa, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and reach their full potential.

Who We Are

Founded in 2022, we are a passionate group of advocates, volunteers, and donors united by a shared vision: a brighter future for children in Africa. Our diverse team includes dedicated professionals committed to making a lasting impact.

What We Do

  • Education: Providing access to quality education and resources.
  • Healthcare: Offering essential medical services and health education.
  • Empowerment: Supporting children programs that foster talents and skills in all aspects.
